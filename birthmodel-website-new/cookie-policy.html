Cookie Policy

Last Updated: July 2025

About This Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Birth Model, Inc. uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website. It covers what cookies are, why we use them, and how you can control them.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

There are two types of cookies:

  • First-party cookies are set by the website you are visiting
  • Third-party cookies are set by other parties, enabling features like advertising and analytics

Why We Use Cookies

Birth Model uses cookies for several purposes:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the technical operation of our website ("strictly necessary" cookies)
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website
  • Functionality cookies: Remember your preferences and enhance your experience
  • Marketing cookies: Track your interests and measure advertising effectiveness

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have several options for managing cookies:

Cookie Consent Manager

You can manage your preferences through our Cookie Consent Manager, which allows you to accept or reject different cookie categories. Note that essential cookies cannot be rejected as they are necessary for the website to function.

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can typically:

  • View what cookies are stored on your device
  • Delete all or specific cookies
  • Block all cookies or cookies from specific sites
  • Block third-party cookies

Please note that blocking all cookies may affect the functionality of our website and other sites you visit.

Opt-Out Tools

You can also opt out of certain advertising cookies through industry tools such as:

  • Digital Advertising Alliance (DAA)
  • Network Advertising Initiative (NAI)

Web Beacons and Tracking Pixels

In addition to cookies, we may use web beacons (also known as "tracking pixels"). These are tiny graphics with unique identifiers that allow us to:

  • Monitor traffic patterns on our website
  • Track email campaign success
  • Measure website performance

Flash Cookies

Some features may use Flash cookies (Local Shared Objects or LSOs). These are different from browser cookies and may not be removed by standard browser controls. You can manage Flash cookie settings through:

  • Flash Player Website Storage Settings Panel
  • Global Storage Settings Panel

Targeted Advertising

Third parties may serve cookies for advertising purposes based on your browsing history across multiple websites. These cookies help deliver more relevant ads to you. You can opt out of targeted advertising through the tools mentioned above.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legislation, or our data practices. We encourage you to periodically review this page for the latest information.
