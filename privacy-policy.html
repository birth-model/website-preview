Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Summary of Key Points

This privacy policy outlines our data collection practices, processing purposes, sharing procedures, security measures, and your rights regarding personal information.

Introduction

Birth Model, Inc. ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website at www.birthmodel.com, use our mobile application, or access our services.

Personal Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal information:

Contact Information

  • Names and phone numbers
  • Email addresses and mailing addresses

Account Information

  • Usernames and passwords
  • Job titles and professional credentials

Technical Data

  • Device IDs and IP addresses
  • Operating system information
  • Browser type and version

Sensitive Information (with consent)

  • Health data
  • Race and ethnicity information
  • Government identifiers

Mobile Permissions

  • Bluetooth access
  • SMS capabilities
  • Storage access
  • Sensor data

How We Use Your Information

We process your information to:

  • Create and maintain user accounts
  • Deliver requested services
  • Respond to inquiries and provide customer support
  • Send administrative communications
  • Manage orders and process payments
  • Facilitate user-to-user communication
  • Evaluate and improve our services
  • Comply with legal obligations

How We Share Your Information

We may share your information with:

  • Business partners and affiliates: To provide integrated services
  • Third parties during business transfers: In connection with mergers, acquisitions, or asset sales
  • Other platform users: When you post in public areas
  • Service providers: Who assist with our operations under confidentiality agreements

Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy. Personal information is retained for a maximum of twenty-four (24) months past account termination unless legally required otherwise.

Security Measures

Birth Model implements technical and organizational safeguards to protect your personal information. However, please note that no electronic transmission over the Internet or information storage technology can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Your Privacy Rights

All Users

You have the right to:

  • Review and update your account information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Disable cookies through browser settings
  • Contact us for privacy inquiries

California Residents

Under the "Shine The Light" law, California residents may request information about third-party data sharing practices once per calendar year.

Virginia Residents

Under the Consumer Data Protection Act (CDPA), Virginia residents have additional rights including:

  • Access to personal data
  • Correction of inaccuracies
  • Deletion requests
  • Opt-out from targeted advertising
  • Data portability

Data Access Requests

To exercise your data access rights, please submit a request through our data subject access form or contact us at support@birthmodel.com.

Policy Updates

We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. We will notify users of material changes through our website or direct communication. Your continued use of our services after such modifications constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
