Our Story

The birth of Birth Model.

"During my OB-GYN residency, I witnessed the chaos of filled labor rooms, care delays, and non-labor patients occupying critical spaces. I quickly realized the need for a more organized and predictable birthing process.

We created Birth Model to use patient data and machine learning to predict delivery times, optimize staffing, and reduce patient wait times. Over the years, our platform has immensely synchronized hospital operations, saved staffing costs, increased L&D revenue, reduced provider burnout, boosted patient satisfaction, and lowered primary cesarean rates—all while seamlessly integrating with Epic workflows."

— Anish J. Shah, MD, FACOG, Founder and CEO of Birth Model
